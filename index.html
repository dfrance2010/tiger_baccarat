Welcome to Tiger Baccarat training

How to play:

  • Hit 'Play Game' to start.
  • The cards will deal themselves.
  • After the hand, take down the losing bets by tapping each loser. Leave winning bets orange.
  • After taking the losers, hit the 'Submit Answer' button.
  • A message will tell you if you're correct or not.
  • That's it. Have fun!
