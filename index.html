Welcome to Tiger Baccarat practice

How to play:

  • Hit 'Play Game' to start.
  • The cards will deal themselves.
  • After the hand, take down the losing bets by tapping each loser. Leave winning bets orange.
  • After taking the losers, hit the 'Submit Answer' button.
  • If there are no bets to pay, that's it.
  • If there are bets to pay, tap each one to pay, starting on the left.
  • A payout screen with a breakdown of the bet will appear. Enter your answer using as many of the answer fields as you need. The amounts will automatically add together when you pay the bet, so you can break down the bet however you like.
  • When you think you have the right answer, or combination of payouts that will add to the right answer, it the 'Pay Bet' button.
  • Keep paying until you've paid all the winners.
  • $5 chips are sometimes included because you have to pay yourself. Make that money
  • The default setting is a random 8-deck shoe and represents a real game. If you want to have more tiger winners come up, click on the "Give me more Tigers" button.
  • That's it. Have fun!
